Plot Booking

In this application we are not only changing the status of a plot, we can also store every information regarding the sales for future use. Who bought the plot ? - Customer Who brokered the deal ? - Agent / Broker What brokerage / incentive did we offer ? - Brokerage / Incentive Who registered the booking ? - Employee / Partner

Transactions

Be it a 1/4th payment or an emi installment, all payments of a booking can be recorded in the system.
